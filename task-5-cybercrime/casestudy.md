# Task 5 – Cybercrime Case Study

## Topic: UPI / Online Payment Fraud

As a Student Digital Ambassador at VIT Bhopal University, 
I want my  batchmates to be aware of UPI fraud — one of 
the fastest growing cybercrimes targeting college students
in India.

## What is UPI Fraud?

UPI fraud is a crime where scammers trick people into 
revealing their UPI PIN or approving fake payment requests 
through calls or messages. Once the money is sent it is 
nearly impossible to recover.

## How it Happens

A scammer calls pretending to be a bank agent or online 
buyer. They gain your trust using basic details like your 
name or bank name. They then ask you to scan a QR code or 
enter your UPI PIN to "receive" money. The moment you do 
this money is instantly debited and the scammer disappears.

## Who is Targeted?

First year students selling items on OLX or Instagram, new 
UPI users and anyone who is in a hurry or under pressure 
during the call.

## Real Scenario

A student posted his laptop for sale on OLX. A buyer sent 
a QR code saying "scan to receive payment." The student 
scanned it, entered his PIN and lost Rs. 15,000 instantly. 
The scammer switched off his phone immediately after.

## Consequences

Financial loss, mental stress, embarrassment and repeated 
targeting by scammers.

If this happens — report immediately at cybercrime.gov.in 
or call helpline 1930.
