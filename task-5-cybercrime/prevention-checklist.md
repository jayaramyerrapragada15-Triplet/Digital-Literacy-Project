# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
### For College Students in India

---

## General Online Safety

- Never share your passwords with anyone. Not even 
  your closest friends

- Use different passwords for different accounts and 
  change them every few months

- Always check the URL of a website before entering 
  any personal or banking details

- Do not click on links sent through unknown SMS, 
  WhatsApp messages or emails

- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all 
  important accounts

- Keep your phone and apps updated — updates often 
  fix security vulnerabilities

## UPI & Financial Safety 

- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money — 
  PIN is only needed when sending money, never 
  when receiving

- Never scan a QR code sent by a stranger to 
  receive a payment — *QR codes only send money*, 
  they never receive it

- Do not share OTP, CVV or card details with 
  anyone over call or chat even if they claim 
  to be from your bank

- Always verify the UPI ID or phone number 
  before sending any payment

## Reporting a Cybercrime 

- Report any cybercrime immediately at:
   -> cybercrime.gov.in

- Call the National Cyber Crime Helpline:
   -> 1930 (available 24x7)

- Do not wait or feel embarrassed — faster 
  reporting increases chances of recovery
  
---

*Remember — no legitimate bank, company or person will 
ever ask for your PIN, OTP or password. Ever.*
